Lasagna

Description

Really simple lasagna recipe, you can add more ingrediets if you wish! such as broccoli, onions and almost any vegetable you can think of!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Place layer of pasta followed by meat, cheese and tomato sauce
  4. Repeat this for 5 layers
  5. Put it in the oven until the cheese completely melts
